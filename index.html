What is LunaChibiNFT?


LunaChibi are 5000 randomly generated NFTs, that are the cutest beings on the Terra blockchain. It is the answer to this lonely and dark universe - the place that surrounds us all and devours everyone like a black hole.


Those tiny little creatures are the light in the tunnel, sparkles that can overcome any obstacle and grant eternal happiness. Their task is to entertain you, make you admire them as often as possible and create the joy that you can share with other people.

Unique cool design


Our LunaChibis are made by talented artist that creates their every artwork with much needed attention to detail.


Is there anyone that will not be astonished by their charm? You do not need to treat them only as a way of quick earnings. They can become your cute companions, virtual friends, that accompany you throughout your adventures.
